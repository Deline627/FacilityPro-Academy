STAGE 4 — SERVICE DELIVERY

Dry workflow — General Cleaning SOP

Stage sequence: Dry → Kitchens → Bathrooms → Glass → Mopping
8-step general cleaning procedure — execute in order, every time, no exceptions
BLUE cloth — general
FacilityPro Academy | v4.0
Laminate and display on site
NO DUST STANDARD — All surfaces must be visibly dust-free and presentation-ready before leaving area
PREP
1
Clear and prepare work area
Remove obstacles from all surfaces. Organise workspace for unobstructed access. Prepare all cleaning tools — BLUE microfibre cloths, vacuum, and dusting equipment.
2
Empty and reline all bins
Empty all general waste and recycling bins. Do not mix waste streams. Replace with correct-size liners. Wipe bin exteriors. Position bins off floor (e.g. on chairs) for vacuuming access. Return to marked positions after vacuuming.
CLEAN
3
Dust all surfaces — high to low, no exceptions BLUE cloth
Dust all surfaces top-down: shelving and high ledges first, then desk surfaces and monitor stands, then chair rails and skirting boards. Use dry BLUE microfibre cloth. Work high to low to prevent re-contamination.
4
Wipe and polish all surfaces — no smears or residue BLUE cloth
Wipe all desk surfaces, counters, and fixtures using correct cloth and product. Ensure no smears, marks, or product residue remain. Buff to a clean, dry finish.
5
Detail all high-touch points
Clean and disinfect all high-touch surfaces: door handles, light switches, phones, keyboards, touch panels, handrails, and shared equipment. Work left to right or clockwise — do not clean randomly. Do not skip any contact point.
6
Vacuum all areas — edges first, then open floor, then under desks
Vacuum edges, corners, and under desks first using crevice attachment. Then vacuum open areas in straight, overlapping passes. Do not vacuum over cables — move first, replace after. Do not vacuum wet spills.
Complete dry workflow before moving to wet stages
FINISH
7
Reset and align workspace to presentation standard
Tuck all chairs in neatly — consistent height and alignment. Align stationery and desk items. Straighten cushions and soft furnishings. Return all moved items to exact original positions.
8
Restore layout, complete final inspection, and sign off
Return bins to marked positions. Confirm all cables replaced after vacuuming. Conduct final visual inspection of all surfaces under available light. Sign off area before proceeding to wet workflow.
Non-negotiable rules
• Bins always first — no exceptions
• Always work top to bottom
• Do NOT skip high-touch points
• Do NOT vacuum before dusting
• Edges and corners must be completed
• Do NOT leave smears or dust residue
• Replace cloth if dirty or ineffective
• Maintain consistent movement pattern
Final verification — before leaving area
All bins emptied, relined, and positioned correctly
Surfaces dust-free — no visible debris
No dust visible on ledges, desk edges, or skirting
Desks clean and correctly arranged
High-touch points cleaned
No smears or residue on surfaces
Edges and corners completed
Floors fully vacuumed (incl. under desks)
Area visually clean and presentation-ready
