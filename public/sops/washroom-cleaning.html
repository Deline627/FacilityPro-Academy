STAGE 4 — SERVICE DELIVERY

Wet workflow — Washroom SOP

Stage sequence: Kitchens → Bathrooms → Glass → Mopping
8-step washroom procedure — execute in order, every time, no exceptions
RED = toilets
YELLOW = sinks
WHITE = glass
FacilityPro Academy | v4.0
Laminate and display on site
DWELL TIME REQUIRED — Minimum 60 seconds contact before wiping any sanitised surface
PREP
1
Clear and prepare washroom area
Remove all waste. Restock consumables if required (toilet paper, soap, hand towels). Prepare all cleaning tools and confirm correct colour-coded cloths are available.
2
Empty and reline all bins
Empty all washroom bins. Replace with correct-size liners. Wipe bin exteriors. Return bins to marked positions.
CLEAN
3
Apply sanitiser to all toilet areas (observe dwell time) RED cloth
Clean high to low: cistern first, then seat (top and underside), then bowl exterior and interior rim, then surrounding surfaces. Use RED cloth only — toilets and urinals. Allow full 60-second dwell time before wiping.
Dwell time: min 60 sec contact before wiping
4
Apply sanitiser to sinks and fixtures (observe dwell time) YELLOW cloth
Sanitise all sinks, taps, counters, and splashbacks. Use YELLOW cloth only — sinks and fixtures. Allow full 60-second dwell time before wiping. Finish dry — no water marks or residue on any surface.
Dwell time: min 60 sec contact before wiping
5
Clean mirrors and glass WHITE cloth
Wipe all mirrors and glass surfaces using WHITE microfibre cloth and glass cleaner. Spray sparingly. Wipe in overlapping vertical passes. Check at 45-degree angle in natural light before moving on.
6
Replenish all consumables
Check and restock: toilet paper, hand soap dispensers, hand towels or paper products. Confirm all dispensers are functional and at correct levels. Report any empty or faulty dispensers via ConnectTeams.
Complete washroom before moving to next area
FINISH
7
Mop all washroom floors last — do not re-enter area after mopping
Mop entire washroom floor using correct floor cleaner. Work back-to-front toward exit. Rinse mop head every 2–3 passes. Place wet floor signs before mopping — remove only when fully dry.
8
Restore layout, complete final verification, and sign off
Confirm all fixtures, dispensers, and bins are in original positions. Conduct final visual inspection of all surfaces under available light. Check for odour — space must smell neutral. Sign off washroom zone before proceeding.
Non-negotiable rules
• RED cloth = toilets and urinals ONLY
• YELLOW cloth = sinks and fixtures ONLY
• NEVER mix cloths between zones
• Observe dwell time — do not wipe early
• Mop last — never before surfaces complete
• Use wet floor signs at all times during mopping
• Do NOT leave floors or fixtures wet
• Do NOT cross-contaminate equipment
• Do NOT use toilet cloths on any other surface
Final verification — before leaving washroom
All toilet surfaces sanitised and dry
Sinks and taps clean — no residue
Mirrors streak-free (45° angle check)
Floors clean, dry, no pooling
Bins relined and positioned correctly
Consumables fully stocked
No odour — neutral and clean
Surfaces pass visual inspection under light
No splash marks around toilet base or floor
All items returned to correct position
