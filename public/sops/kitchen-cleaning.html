STAGE 4 — SERVICE DELIVERY

Wet workflow — Kitchen SOP

Stage sequence: Kitchens → Bathrooms → Glass → Mopping
8-step kitchen procedure — execute in order, every time, no exceptions
GREEN cloth only
FacilityPro Academy | v4.0
Laminate and display on site
DWELL TIME REQUIRED — Minimum 30 seconds contact before wiping any sanitised surface
PREP
1
Clear and prepare workspace
Remove all loose items from countertops. Gather all required cleaning supplies and GREEN cloths. Clear the full kitchen workspace before any cleaning begins.
2
Empty and reline all bins
Empty all refuse and recycling bins. Do not mix waste streams. Replace with correct-size liners. Wipe bin exteriors. Return bins to marked positions.
CLEAN
3
Wash, dry, and store all dishes
Wash all pots, pans, and utensils. Load dishwasher if in scope. Dry all clean items and return to designated storage. Leave sink clear for Step 6.
4
Apply sanitiser to all counters and surfaces (observe dwell time)
Apply sanitiser to all countertops, tables, and islands using GREEN cloth. Lift and clean under small appliances. Wipe interior of microwave. Allow full dwell time before wiping — do not wipe early.
Dwell time: min 30 sec contact before wiping
5
Wipe all appliance exteriors
Wipe exterior of microwave, refrigerator, kettle, and all smaller appliances. Remove fingerprints and smears. Replace kitchen towels or sponges if required.
6
Clean and sanitise sink, taps, and drainer
Clean sink basin, drainer, and all tap handles using kitchen cleaner. Apply sanitiser and observe dwell time. Buff taps dry — no water marks or residue. Remove all grease and grime.
Complete kitchen before moving to bathrooms
FINISH
7
Sweep and mop all kitchen floors
Sweep entire kitchen floor to remove all debris. Mop using correct floor cleaner and figure-8 motion. Work back-to-front toward exit. Place wet floor sign. Change mop water after kitchen area.
8
Restore layout, complete final verification, and sign off
Return all items removed in Step 1 to original positions. Remove refuse bags to waste point. Confirm lights and switches set to pre-clean state. Conduct final visual inspection of all surfaces under available light. Sign off kitchen zone before proceeding.
Non-negotiable rules
• GREEN cloth only in kitchen areas
• Do NOT use RED or YELLOW cloths in kitchen
• Never mix kitchen and bathroom cloths
• Observe dwell time — do not wipe early
• Mopping is always the last task
• Do not open fridges unless in scope
• Change mop water after kitchen floors
• Do NOT leave surfaces wet after cleaning
Final verification — before leaving kitchen
All surfaces clean and dry — no residue
Surfaces pass visual inspection under light
No chemical odour — space smells neutral
Sink clean and dry — taps polished
Bins relined and wiped — in correct position
Appliance exteriors wiped — no fingerprints
Floor mopped and dry — no streaks or pooling
Tea/coffee station neat and items aligned
All items returned to original positions
